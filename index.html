Motion-to-Motion Model

Red: original trajectory; Blue: reconstructed trajectory

What's this?

This is an example of sequence-to-sequence (seq2seq) models for motions. Pushing the above "start" button, a random trajectory, consisting of an x-y position and angle, is generated and a seq2seq model tries to reconstruct it.

Yes, the current version isn't much of anything. It's a practice. I'll make it more awesome soon.
